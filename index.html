AI Lead Qualification Assistant

An automated helper that receives inbound website signups, analyzes their information instantly, and automatically schedules booking calls with qualified business prospects.

Problem Inbound leads sit for hours before outreach, causing clients to lose interest.
Solution An automated workflow evaluates lead details and routes them immediately.
Outcome Form response times dropped under five minutes with zero manual triage work.

The Challenge

Many businesses receive hundreds of inbound inquiries every day. Sales teams spend hours sorting through spam and low-quality leads, delaying response times to serious potential clients.

The Approach

This demo system automatically monitors incoming signups. It analyzes prospect details against specific criteria to instantly separate qualified leads from spam.

The Implementation

We set up an automation flow that triggers whenever a new form is submitted. The AI evaluates the lead details and automatically updates a central tracker while sending a calendar booking link to qualified users.

System Performance

The system processes submissions in under a minute. It runs continuously in the background, allowing teams to instantly engage high-quality prospects without doing any manual sorting.
